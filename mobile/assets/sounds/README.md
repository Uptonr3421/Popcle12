# Sound Assets

Add the following audio files here to enable sound feedback:

- `beep-success.mp3` — Short positive beep (~0.3s) for successful stamp scan
- `beep-error.mp3`   — Short negative tone (~0.3s) for scan errors
- `celebration.mp3`  — Happy jingle (~1s) for when customer reaches 10 stamps

## Free Sources
- https://freesound.org (CC0 licensed sounds)
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/ (free tier)
- Record your own via any audio app

Once files are added, uncomment the audio lines in `mobile/lib/feedback.ts`.
