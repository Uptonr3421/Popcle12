## Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
Pop Culture CLE is the official loyalty app for Pop Culture CLE — Cleveland's favorite pop-art themed ice cream and sweets shop!

EARN STAMPS, GET FREE TREATS
Every visit gets you closer to free ice cream, popsicles, and sweet treats. Show your QR code at the counter, collect stamps on your digital stamp card, and watch your rewards stack up. It's the easiest loyalty program in Cleveland — no paper punch cards to lose, no codes to remember.

EXCLUSIVE DEALS & OFFERS
Get access to deals you won't find anywhere else. Flash sales, seasonal specials, BOGO pops, and limited-time discounts drop right into the app. Be the first to know when something sweet is happening.

LOCATION-BASED SURPRISES
Walking near Pop Culture CLE? The app detects when you're close and sends you a special offer on the spot. Geofenced deals mean the closer you get, the sweeter the rewards.

PUSH NOTIFICATIONS YOU ACTUALLY WANT
New flavor drop? Half-price sundaes this Saturday? Holiday hours? Get timely updates and never miss a deal. You control what notifications you receive.

YOUR LOYALTY DASHBOARD
Track your stamp count, see your reward history, and know exactly how close you are to your next free treat. Everything is real-time — stamp added at the counter, instantly on your phone.

FAST & EASY SIGN-UP
Create your account in seconds with your phone number. No passwords to forget, no forms to fill out. Just verify and start earning.

BUILT FOR CLEVELAND
Pop Culture CLE is a proud Cleveland and Solon, Ohio small business. This app is built to give back to our loyal customers who make the shop what it is. Every stamp is a thank you.

FEATURES AT A GLANCE
- Digital stamp card with real-time updates
- Personal QR code for fast check-ins
- Exclusive deals and limited-time offers
- Geofenced location-based rewards
- Push notifications for new deals and flavors
- Reward history and transaction tracking
- Simple phone number sign-up

Whether you're grabbing a scoop after work, treating the kids on a Saturday, or hunting for the perfect dessert spot in the Cleveland area — Pop Culture CLE rewards you for every visit.

Download now and start earning your way to free ice cream!
```
